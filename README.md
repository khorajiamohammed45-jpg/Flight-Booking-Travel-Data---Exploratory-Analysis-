# Flight-Booking-Travel-Data---Exploratory-Analysis-
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built using flight booking and travel data to analyze customer behavior, booking trends, and travel patterns. The dashboard transforms raw data into meaningful visual insights, enabling data-driven decision-making for the travel and aviation industry.
This Flight Booking & Travel Data – Exploratory Analysis Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of airline booking performance, passenger demographics, revenue generation, and customer payment preferences. The dashboard summarizes key business metrics, including 250 total bookings, approximately $644K in ticket revenue, 1.89 million kilometers traveled, an average passenger age of 45.6 years, an average flight duration of 11.04 hours, and an average ticket price of $2.58K. These KPIs help stakeholders quickly assess overall business performance and travel activity.

The analysis highlights revenue contributions by airlines and departure cities. B Airways generated the highest ticket revenue, followed by S Airlines and E Airlines, while Mumbai emerged as the top revenue-generating departure city, followed by London and Delhi. The dashboard also reveals customer travel preferences, showing strong booking volumes across Premium Economy, Business, First Class, and Economy classes. These insights help airlines optimize route planning, pricing strategies, and seat allocation based on customer demand.

Additionally, the dashboard examines payment behavior and passenger demographics. Credit Cards and Net Banking contributed the highest ticket revenue, indicating a strong preference for digital payment methods. The age-based analysis shows that middle-aged passengers generate a significant portion of ticket revenue, making them an important customer segment for targeted marketing campaigns. Overall, the dashboard enables data-driven decision-making by identifying revenue opportunities, customer trends, and operational improvements within the airline industry.
